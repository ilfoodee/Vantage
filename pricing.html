Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and services.

Do you charge a call-out fee? keyboard_arrow_down
For standard repairs within our primary service area, we do not charge a separate call-out fee. The first hour of labor is included in the base price of £99. For emergency calls outside of business hours, a surcharge may apply, which will be communicated upfront.
Are parts included in the Annual Service Plan? keyboard_arrow_down
The Annual Service Plan covers labor for the annual service check and minor adjustments. Replacement parts are not included free of charge, but plan members receive a significant 20% discount on all parts required for any repairs.
How do I get a quote for a full installation? keyboard_arrow_down
Because every home is unique, we provide custom quotes for full installations. You can book a free consultation online. One of our engineers will visit your property, assess your heating needs, and provide a detailed, fixed-price quote with no obligation.
What payment methods do you accept? keyboard_arrow_down
We accept all major credit and debit cards, bank transfers, and cash. For larger installations, we also offer flexible financing options to spread the cost over 12, 24, or 36 months.

Still have questions?

Our team is ready to help you find the perfect heating solution for your home or business. We cover the greater metro area.
